Questions/need:
What is the framing question of your analysis, or the purpose of the model/system you plan to build?
Does a large quantity of mentions of a ticker symbol on the subreddit Wall Street Bets influence the volatility of a stock? (Normal volatility will be accounted for using realized volatility)

Who benefits from exploring this question or building this model/system?
Investors of these stocks could gain insight into the behavior of said stock.

Data Description:
What dataset(s) do you plan to use, and how will you obtain the data?
Scraped reddit comments either using slenium or beautiful soup, may resort to using Praw depending on difficulty.
Scraped information of ticker symbols and associated company names using selenium or beautiful soup.
Yfinance api for obtaining market info of tickers.

What is an individual sample/unit of analysis in this project? What characteristics/features do you expect to work with?
Table 1: Ticker symbol, number of mentions, realized volatility, timeframe
Table 2: Ticker symbol, company name

If modeling, what will you predict as your target?
A correlation between volatilty and number of mentions of said stock.

Tools:
How do you intend to meet the tools requirement of the project?
Use beautiful soup and/or Selenium to scrape data

Are you planning in advance to need or use additional tools beyond those required?
Yes, various apis mentioned above.

MVP Goal:
What would a minimum viable product (MVP) look like for this project?
A linear regression model between the most mentioned stock and volatilty occured by stock over a 1 week (5 trading days) period.
